# Color Pairing

Fidely UI lets you combine neutral and accent tones to shape your brand’s unique visual style.
By default, the system pairs neutral as both the gray and accent base, giving a clean, minimal look out of the box.

However, you can easily swap in any color from the Fidely palette to craft a personality that fits your product.
